Gently cleanse and strengthen your hair with our nutrient-rich shampoo formula.

Massage 2–3 pumps into wet hair and scalp. Work into a rich lather, rinse thoroughly. Repeat if needed.

What It Does

  • • Strengthens roots and repairs hair fibers
  • • Boosts shine and elasticity

Key Ingredients

  • • Aloe Vera: Hydrates scalp and soothes irritation
  • • Argan Oil: Nourishes and protects against dryness
  • • Rosemary Oil: Stimulates scalp circulation and promotes growth
  • • Vitamin E & B5: Support healthy, resilient hair
  • • Silk Protein: Improves softness and shine

Hydrating Conditioner

  • • Enhances shine and manageability

Key Ingredients

  • • Vitamin B5: Locks in hydration and adds smoothness
